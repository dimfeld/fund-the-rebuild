Why this exists

In the wake of a disaster, many charitable camapaigns spring up on GoFundMe. Some go viral and exceed their goals by a large margin. Others may raise a bit from friends but are mostly forgotten.

During the spring of 2020, looting across the USA took everything from some people and small business owners. This site exists to help them in a small way, and bring attention to the charitable campaigns that need some extra visibility to reach their goals.

It by no means solves the larger problems around the situation, but I hope this can help bring some good to people who are suffering.

How to help

Donate to one of the causes here! All links go directly to GoFundMe. I get no percentage or any other consideration.

If you know of a GoFundMe campaign that should be listed here, you can click the "Add" link at the top and submit it. I'm currently processing those submissions manually myself, so it may take a while but I try to get to them within a day.
